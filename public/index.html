Cardio & Fat Loss Plan

Weekly cardio structure
Thu (gym): HIIT — rowing + sled intervals. 25 min structured. Burns ~250–280 kcal.
Sat/Sun (outdoor): Cycling or running 45–60 min. Zone 2 HR (60–70% max = ~130–145 bpm). Burns ~300–400 kcal/session.
Post-strength (optional): Mon/Tue/Fri: 15 min rower/elliptical at Zone 2 after session. ~150 kcal without impairing recovery.
Weekly target: ~1000–1400 kcal from cardio. Combined with ~400 kcal food deficit = ~1.5 kg fat/month.
HIIT protocol (Thursday)
Protocol: Work 30 s / Rest 90 s × 8 rounds (rower). RPE 9/10 on work intervals.
Why rowing: Full body, low joint impact, burns trunk fat effectively — directly addresses your high abdominal fat.
Sled push: 20 m × 4. Heavy enough that last 5 m is hard. Pure power + calorie burn.
Progression: Add 1 round every 2 weeks. After 8 weeks, reduce rest to 60 s. Track HR recovery — should improve over 4–6 weeks.
